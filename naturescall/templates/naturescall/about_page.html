About us

Hello, world. This is Nature's Call, an app that's created to help people with their basic, urgent need when they're outside of their house and when available restroom is hard to find. We notice that in NYC, it's hard to find restroom that is easily accessible. For this reason, we decided to make a community-based app allowing poeple to pinpoint the places that can be potential spots for restroom and help out others in this community to easily find the restrooms in NYC.
